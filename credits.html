Sources and Credits

This website was created by Ayaan Sunesara, a student at Travis High School. The data used for each of the visualizations was collected and compiled from the following sources:

  • Correctional Population Over Time: "Number of persons supervised by adult correctional systems in the U.S., by correctional status, 1980-2020". Bureau of Justice Statistics, Annual Probation Survey, Annual Parole, Survey, Annual Survey of Jails, Census of Jails, and National Prisoner Statistics Program. Date of version March 2022.
  • Drug Arrests for Black Population By Year: "Arrest in the United States, 1980-2014". Bureau of Justice Statistics, FBI, Uniform Crime Reporting Program. Date of version Aug 2017.
  • Population vs State Prison Population By Race: "Beyond the count: A deep dive into state prison populations". Leah Wang, Wendy Sawyer, Tiana Herring, and Emily Widra. Date of Version April 2022.
  • Black Population Percentage vs Black Drug Arrest Rate 1980-2014: "Arrest in the United States, 1980-2014". Bureau of Justice Statistics, FBI, Uniform Crime Reporting Program. Date of version Aug 2017.
